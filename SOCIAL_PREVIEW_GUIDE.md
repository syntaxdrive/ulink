## Why You Need This

When someone shares your UniLink URL on social media (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp), a **preview card** appears. This card can make or break whether people click. A premium preview increases clicks by **up to 300%**!

## What You Need to Create

### 1. **Open Graph Image** (Primary - Most Important!)
- **Dimensions:** 1200 x 630 px (Facebook/LinkedIn/WhatsApp standard)
- **File format:** PNG or JPG
- **File size:** Under 1MB (ideally 300-500KB)
- **File name:** `og-image.png`
- **Safe zone:** Keep important content within central 1200 x 600 px

### 2. **Twitter Image** (Optional but recommended)
- **Dimensions:** 1200 x 675 px (16:9 ratio)
- **File format:** PNG or JPG
- **File size:** Under 5MB
- **File name:** `twitter-card.png`

### Design Principles

1. **Typography:**
   - Use **Space Grotesk** or **Outfit** (same as your site)
   - Make "UniLink" huge and bold (72-96px)
   - Keep tagline readable (36-48px)
   - Maximum 2-3 lines of text

2. **Visuals:**
   - Add subtle geometric patterns
   - Use gradient backgrounds (emerald to teal)
   - Include icons: graduation cap, network nodes, chat bubbles
   - Apply glassmorphism or subtle shadows for depth

3. **Safe Zone:**
   - Keep critical content 100px from edges
   - Some platforms crop differently
   - Test on mobile preview (some platforms show circular crops)

4. **Branding:**
   - Include your logo (top-left or center)
   - Use consistent colors with your app
   - Make it instantly recognizable

## Testing Your Preview Card

After uploading, test how it looks:

1. **Facebook/LinkedIn Debugger:**
   - [Facebook Debugger](https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/)
   - Paste your URL
   - Click "Scrape Again"
   - See preview

2. **Twitter Card Validator:**
   - [Twitter Card Validator](https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator)
   - Paste your URL
   - See how it renders

3. **General OG Preview:**
   - [OpenGraph.xyz](https://www.opengraph.xyz/)
   - Shows previews for all platforms

4. **Mobile Preview:**
   - [Metatags.io](https://metatags.io/)
   - Shows how it looks on mobile
