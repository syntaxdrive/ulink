## 1. ABOUT UNILINK NIGERIA

**UniLink Nigeria** is a mobile and web application developed by SyntaxDrive Technologies, combining social networking, academic resource sharing, career tools, and AI-powered learning — built specifically for the Nigerian campus experience.

**Mission:** Connect every Nigerian student to the tools, knowledge, and people they need to thrive in university and beyond.

**Available on:**
- Android (mobile app)
- Web — unilink.ng (installable as a PWA on any smartphone, no app store needed)

**The four pillars of UniLink:**

| Pillar | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Academic Excellence | Share and discover course materials with AI tutoring |
| Campus Community | Connect with students across Nigeria |
| Career Readiness | Jobs, internships, talent discovery, AI resume review |
| Campus Life | News, podcasts, challenges, marketplace, study rooms |

## 2. THE PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING

Nigerian students navigate a deeply fragmented digital experience:

- Course materials shared in WhatsApp groups that archive and disappear
- Job opportunities missed because no central board exists
- Union announcements competing with noise on Instagram and Twitter
- Academic support that is expensive, inaccessible, or both
- No platform that understands the Nigerian campus experience

For the UI Student Union specifically, the challenge is governance at scale — communicating reliably with over 20,000 students across faculties, departments, and year groups, using tools (WhatsApp broadcasts, notice boards, social media) that were never designed for that purpose.

UniLink is built to close all of these gaps in one place.

## 3. HOW UNILINK WORKS

### Student Onboarding — Step by Step

| Step | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Sign in with Google — no new password required |
| 2 | Select your university (University of Ibadan is listed) |
| 3 | Choose your role: Student or Organisation |
| 4 | Create a username |
| 5 | Connect with fellow students before entering the app |
| 6 | Full access to the platform from day one |

**Sign Up & Onboarding**
Students use their existing Google account — no new password needed. They pick their university, choose a role (Student or Organisation), and set a username. Before entering the app, new users are guided to connect with fellow students so the experience feels alive from day one.

### Verification System

| Type | Badge | Who Gets It |
|---|---|---|
| Blue Verified | 🔵 | Standard accounts in good standing |
| Gold Verified | 🟡 | Student leaders, notable individuals, organisations, and the SUG |

**University-Scoped Experience**
Feeds, communities, and talent searches can all be filtered by university — UI students always surface content relevant to their campus, while still being connected to the full national network.

**Real-Time Infrastructure**
Posts, messages, poll votes, and leaderboard changes update instantly across all devices. No refresh needed.

#### UAI — Universal AI Instructor

**How UAI works — step by step:**

| Step | What Happens |
|---|---|
| 1 | Student opens a course document (e.g. past exam questions) |
| 2 | Taps the UAI floating button on screen |
| 3 | Types or speaks a question about the material |
| 4 | UAI scans the document and retrieves only the relevant sections |
| 5 | A full, contextual answer is delivered instantly — in text or by voice |
| 6 | Student continues with follow-up questions in the same session |

**UAI capabilities:**
- Answers questions drawn directly from the uploaded document — not generic internet answers
- Voice input and text-to-speech responses
- Image uploads — photograph a textbook page and ask about it
- Conversation history saved per session
- Works across all documents in a course simultaneously
- No extra cost. No external subscription. Free for every student.

### 5.3 What the SUG Should Know

- **No student fees — ever.** The model makes them unnecessary and counterproductive
- **No data selling.** Advertisers pay for reach, not raw personal data. All targeting uses anonymised internal signals
- **Self-reinforcing model.** More students → more attractive to advertisers → more revenue → better product for students
- **UI's contribution counts.** Every student the SUG onboards directly increases the platform's commercial value — which feeds back into better tools for those same students

### 5.4 Revenue Activation Milestone

UniLink will not activate any revenue streams targeting the UI student community until **500 active UI students** are registered on the platform. No sponsored posts, no employer-facing job listings, no brand campaigns — nothing — until that threshold is reached.

This is a deliberate commitment. It means UniLink's first priority on the UI campus is building a genuine, active community — not monetising an empty room. It also means the SUG can promote UniLink to students with confidence: there will be no adverts, no commercial noise, and no distractions in the early days. Just the platform working exactly as described.

Once the 500-student milestone is crossed, revenue streams activate gradually and transparently — starting with job listings from verified employers, which directly benefit the same students on the platform.

## 6. BENEFITS TO THE UI STUDENT UNION

### 6.1 A Reliable Communication Channel

Currently, the SUG has no single channel that verifiably reaches all 20,000+ students. UniLink changes this:

- Gold-verified SUG account posts go directly into every registered UI student's feed
- Faculty and department communities replace scattered WhatsApp groups with structured, searchable, admin-controlled spaces
- Analytics show exactly how many students saw and engaged with each announcement — something no WhatsApp broadcast or Instagram post can provide

### 6.2 Governance and Engagement Tools

- Run campus polls and see real-time results across faculties
- Launch referral campaigns to mobilise participation in elections, events, or awareness drives
- Use the leaderboard to create university-wide competition around academic or civic engagement
- Sponsor campus challenges under the SUG's own brand

### 6.3 A Free Academic Resource for UI Students

Every UI student on UniLink gains:
- Access to the shared course material library built by their peers
- UAI — AI tutoring available 24/7 inside every uploaded document
- High-resolution in-app PDF reading without downloading files or using external apps

Endorsing UniLink is, in practical terms, delivering a free academic tool to every student the SUG represents.

### 6.4 Career Infrastructure for Graduates

- UI students become searchable by verified employers the moment their profile is complete
- Direct access to jobs and internships without relying on third-party job sites
- AI Resume Review before they start applying — structured feedback at no cost

### 6.5 Built for the Nigerian Campus

Unlike adapted foreign platforms, UniLink understands the Nigerian student specifically:
- WhatsApp sharing built into every feature
- Naira-currency marketplace
- NYSC category in the confessions board and news
- Nigerian universities, Nigerian news sources, Nigerian advertisers

### 6.6 Data and Insights

A partnership gives the SUG access to anonymised, aggregated data about UI student behaviour on the platform — what topics generate the most discussion, which content resonates, what opportunities students are actively seeking. Student leadership can make decisions with real information, not guesswork.

### 6.7 National Visibility for UI

- University of Ibadan appearing at the top of a national leaderboard is visible to every student on the platform
- Trending UI hashtags are seen across all universities
- Being UniLink's flagship campus positions the university as digitally progressive — a signal that carries weight with prospective students, employers, and the public

## 7. WHAT THE PARTNERSHIP INCLUDES

This is a single, unconditional partnership offer. There are no tiers, no locked features, and no hidden costs. From the day the partnership is formalised, the UI Student Union receives the following in full:
